About Us

Independent, realistic and student-focused guidance

İtalya Education Services is an independent platform created to provide experience-based guidance to students who are planning to study in Italy.

Our aim is to help students understand and manage their education journey in a more informed, organised and realistic way.

Our online platform is not affiliated with any university, consulate, embassy, government institution, private organisation or individual.

Why Was This Platform Created?

To make scattered information easier to understand

Students planning to study in Italy often encounter scattered, outdated or incomplete information. İtalya Education Services was created to help students access reliable information more easily and manage the process in a more informed way.

Why Choose Us?

Realistic expectations instead of guarantees

Real Experience

Information based on the experiences of people who have studied and lived in Italy.

Student-Focused Approach

Guidance centred on the student's goals, questions and real needs.

Transparent Information

An honest approach that explains both the opportunities and the possible difficulties of the process.

Process Guidance

Support that helps students progress in a more organised way through university research, document preparation and application stages.

For Parents

We value your opinion, but the student remains at the centre

We value the opinions and experiences of parents. However, students remain at the centre of our guidance process. They are the ones who will study in Italy, begin a new life and experience this journey personally. Now it is their turn to begin a new chapter.

For this reason, we encourage students to participate actively, consciously and responsibly throughout the entire process.

Who Is This Platform For?

Who may benefit from our guidance?

  • Students planning to pursue undergraduate and/or master's degree studies in Italy
  • Students researching universities in Italy
  • Students who need help understanding application processes
  • Students looking for practical information about life in Italy

Who Is It Not For?

  • People expecting guaranteed university admission
  • People planning to go to Italy solely for tourism
  • People expecting certainty regarding visa outcomes
  • People expecting guidance that replaces the decisions of official institutions

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak Italian to study in Italy?

Not necessarily. Language requirements depend on the programme. Some programmes are taught in English, while others require Italian. However, speaking Italian can make daily life, social interaction and practical matters considerably easier.

Do you guarantee university admission?

No. Admission decisions are made exclusively by the relevant universities and institutions.

Do you provide official visa consultancy?

No. The information provided is for general guidance and does not constitute official visa or legal consultancy.

Which cities can you provide information about?

We primarily share experience relating to Tuscany, including Pisa, Florence and Siena, as well as general information about student life in Italy.

Can we arrange an online meeting?

You may contact us by email and briefly introduce yourself. Depending on our workload, we generally respond within one business day.
